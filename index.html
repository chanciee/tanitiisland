About Us

Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific. It has sandy and rocky beaches, a small harbor, lush rainforests, and a mountainous interior with an active volcano.

The island has an indigenous population of about 20,000, and until recently, fishing and agriculture were the main industries.
